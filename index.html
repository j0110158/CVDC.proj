CONDITION OF LOANS
  • All loans carry applicable interest rates and service fees.

  • Regular loans are payable at a maximum of ONE HUNDRED EIGHTY (180) days. All other loans have specified periods of payment.

  • A loan becomes delinquent seven (7) days after the specified due date of fully payment. If the loan is not paid within the 7-day grace period, a penalty of two percent (2%) per month is imposed on the remaining balance of the loan.

    • IMPORTANT:
    The purpose in implementing penalties is to rate the member's status in the cooperative and help the borrower to be diligent in repaying loans. Thus, members who fall in their obligation shall be deemed members in bad standing.


  • CO-MAKER/COLLATERAL is a member in good standing and assumes the obligations of the borrower in cases of default to settle the loan, Each Loan Application must have a Co-Maker/ Collateral and consent from immediate family in order to get approved.
