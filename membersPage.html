The membership and subscription agreement shall provide the terms of payment on thr unpaid subscriptions. No member shall own or hold more than 10% of the share capital of the cooperative.
    • A MEMBER IS ENTITLED TO VOTE WHEN
  • He/She has paid the Required Membership fee of One Hundred Pesos and the value of at least One Hundred common shares as provided in the Section 6 of the by-laws
  • He/She is not delinquent in the payment of his capital contribution and accounts;
  • He/She has not violated any provision of the by-laws, rules, and regulations, circulars/memoranda and other issuances of the CDA, membership and subscription agreement and other instruments required of him to execute as provided in the by-laws or as prescribes by the Board of Directors.
  • He/She has ATTENDED AND COMPLETED the prescribed Cooperative Seminars.
  • PATRONIZES regularly the business of the cooperative and participate in its parliamentary affairs;
  • And COMPLIES with all obligations, duties and undertakings of membership.

    • LIABILITY OF MEMBERS
  • The liability of a member shall be limited. No members shall be liable for any indebtedness of the Cooperative beyond his subscribed shares.

    • TERMINATION OF MEMBERSHIP
  • Termination of membership may be automatic, voluntary, or involuntary, which shall have the effect of extinguishing all the rights of the member in the cooperative, subject to refund of share capital contribution under Section 14 of the by-laws.

    • VOLUNTARY TERMINATION
  • Any member m=desiring to terminate his membership by withdrawal shall submit a 60-day written notice to the Board of Directors. However, no member shall be allowed to withdraw or terminate his membership during any period i which he has pending obligation with the cooperative.

    • INVOLUNTARY TERMINATION
  • A member may be terminated by a vote of the majority of the members of the Board of Directors for cause such as non-participation and non-patronage, violation of the by-laws and rules of the cooperative, acts of omission injurious or prejudicial to the interest and welfare of the cooperative.
    • RIGHT AND PRIVILEGES OF A MEMBER
  • REGULAR MEMBERS
    • - ATTEND AND PARTICIPATE in the General Assembly Meetings,
    - VOTE on all matters brought before such meetings(according to the by-laws)
    - AVAIL OF THE SERVICES OF THE COOP without any discrimination whatsoever upon compliance with the conditions and requirements thereof.
    - INSPECT AND EXAMINE the books of accounts, the minutes book, the share register, and other records of the cooperative during office hours and to exercise other rights and privileges of membership.
    - SEEK ANY ELECTIVE POSITIONS subject to the provisions of the by-laws
    - SECURE COPIES OF COOP RECORD/DOCUMENTS pertaining to the account information of the concerned member.
  • ASSOCIATE MEMBERS
    • - Can attend and witness General Assembly meetings but cannot participate, vote, and be voted upon.
    - Other rights and privileges same as listed for Regular Member.
